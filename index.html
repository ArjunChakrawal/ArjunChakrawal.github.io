Biography

I am a doctoral student currently working on scaling problems related to biogeochemical processes in soil carbon cycling models. My goal is to understand the impact of microscale heterogeneities of soil structure on the macroscopic dynamics of microbial decomposition of soil carbon. Currently, I am trying to construct a theoretical model to link the two spatial scales and using hypothetical scenarios to test the model.
